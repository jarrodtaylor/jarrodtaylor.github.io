PayPal 2014
San Jose, CA Principle SDK Engineer

Before NFC payments were a thing, PayPal was branching out into retail and mobile payments by taking advantage of new smartphone capabilities.

I worked with them to develop iOS and Android SDKs for retailers. The SDKs leveraged PayPal services to manage payments, users, locations, products, and shopping sessions.

We used Bluetooth Low Energy to interface with cash registers for in-store digital payments.
